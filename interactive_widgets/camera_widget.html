How to use:
1. Move the Depth [Z] sliders to push the LiDAR points further away.
2. Watch the physical Bounding Box shrink in the Camera View (bottom left) as distance increases.
3. Increase the YOLO Pixel Error to simulate real-world jitter. Notice how the theoretical lateral error (the red error width) explodes for the distant point compared to the close point!
